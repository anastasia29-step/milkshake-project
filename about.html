Built from scratch by our family, for yours. At Heavenly Fresh, every meal is made with love, care, and a little bit of home.

At Heavenly Fresh, we believe healthy food should be simple, delicious, and made with care. As a family-run eatery in Princeton, we’re here to serve fresh, wholesome meals that fit a variety of lifestyles — including vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free options.

From paninis and grain bowls to smoothies and breakfast favorites, we offer something for everyone. Our team is built on kindness, teamwork, and a shared love for great food and community. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or just need a pick-me-up, we're here to make your day a little brighter.
